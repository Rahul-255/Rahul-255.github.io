FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a Capture The Flag competition?

    competition. Contestants are presented with a set of challenges which test their creativity, technical (and googling) skills, and problem-solving ability. Challenges usually cover a number of categories, and when solved, each yields a string (called a flag) which is submitted to an online scoring service. CTFs are a great way to learn a wide array of computer security skills in a safe, legal environment, and are hosted and played by many security groups around the world for fun and practice.

  • Who can play Amrita InCTF International?

    Anyone in the world who is interested in cyber security and CTF

  • How much does it cost to participate in Amrita InCTF International ?

    Participation is fully free: there are no fees for participating in the contest.

  • Why should I participate in this?

    Developing skills in the area of cyber security opens up a whole new range of career opportunities for skilled graduates. Today graduates with expertise in cyber security are in great demand for the positions of security analysts in top private computer firms

  • Where does this happen?

    The Internet! The competition will be indefinitely available online at this website. As long as you have the ability to install software and have unfiltered Internet access, the participants can compete from anywhere

  • What do I need for this competition?

    Full participation in the competition requires only- A computer (preferably running Linux operating system), A modern web browser, the ability to install applications, (and of course) Internet access

  • What are the topics included?

    Our categories are : Reverse engineering Binary exploitation Web exploitation Cryptography Forensics Android Trivia
