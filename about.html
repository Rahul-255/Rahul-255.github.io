About Us

🤔 What is InCTFi?

InCTF International is a Capture The Flag event hosted by teambi0s perennially since 2017, featuring a contest aimed at hackers of all ages around the world to learn, embrace and espouse cyber security.
Since its inception,InCTFi has always stayed one step ahead by featuring challenges to make you think outside the box and stay current. At its core, inCTFi is a jeopardy styled CTF featuring a set of challenges which are provided to the competitors.
We provide challenges in categories like reverse engineering,binary exploitation cryptography, penetration testing, digital forensics and web to accommodate the large and diverse field of cyber security.
InCTFi is not just for the security community. Each year with inCTFi, teambi0s pushes the bounds of exploitation and intellectual limits by moulding state-of-the-art, yet enjoyable challenges, ripped from research papers and public vulnerabilities, to make you the latest and the greatest.
